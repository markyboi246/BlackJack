Blackjack Rules

Objective

The goal of blackjack is to beat the dealer's hand without busting 21.

Card Values

Number Cards are worth Face Value

King, Queen, and Jackare worth 10

Aces are worth either 1 or 11

Initial Gameplay

The game starts by shufflin the cards

Players are dealt two cards that are both face-up, while the dealer gets one face-up and one face-down.

Players are able to bet on the game by clicking the amount of chips that they want to place down.

Players have multiple choices which include:

Hit: Take another card
Stand: Keep your current hand
Double Down: Double your bet, take one more card, and stand
Split: If your first two cards are the same, split them into two separeate hands

If a player's total exceeds 21, they "Bust" and lose the round.

Dealer must hit until their total is at least 17.
